Music

Like most people, music has always been part of my life. But I wasn't happy with just listening to the songs; I wanted to understand everything that was going on there: the notes, harmonics, the music construction itself. So when I was 15 years old, along with my brother and my friend, we decided to study acoustic guitar. A couple of years later, I found out that my dexterity was the electric guitar.

Down here, there are three songs I learned how to play, played by the instructors from whom I learned. One day I intend to replace those videos with others where I'm playing.
